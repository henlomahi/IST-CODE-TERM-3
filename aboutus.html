Rishitha Sankrashiva

Gamer

This was the first game I created, when I was in need. My family was on the poorer side with little entertainment like toys. When I first went into highschool I took coding for the first time just to see what it was. But it then became an output to me, a way to entertain myself. I was able to create websites, games, you name it, just from one device and some typing. I loved it. And people like my friends loved it too, and together we decided that this hobby we have could go far, we can use it to help people like myself to find love it code as well. That was my story :)

Coding was something I forced to be a hobby because I would have never considered it a job option, and this is my message to y'all i'm living proof that coding can be your job regardless of your age, gender or even your status.
